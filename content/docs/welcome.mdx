†

MetaCentrum is a single point of entry to three computing infrastructures:

   * **Grid** (distributed HPC computing based on OpenPBS),
   * **Cloud**, (Openstack cloud computing + CEPH), and
   * **Kubernetes** (containerized computing),

as well as to a number of specialized services, such as <b>Galaxy</b>, <b>OnDemand</b>, and <b>Jupyter notebooks</b>.
