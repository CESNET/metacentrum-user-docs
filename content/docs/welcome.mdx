Welcome to the MetaCentrum documentation, the home of all MetaCentrum services.

<a href="https://www.metacentrum.cz/en">MetaCentrum</a> is a single point of entry to three computing infrastructures:
   * **Grid** (distributed HPC computing based on OpenPBS),
   * **Cloud**, (Openstack cloud computing + CEPH), and
   * **Kubernetes** (containerized computing),
   as well as to a number of more specialized services, such as <b>Elixir</b>, <b>Galaxy</b>, <b>OnDemand</b> and <b>Jupyter notebooks</b>.
