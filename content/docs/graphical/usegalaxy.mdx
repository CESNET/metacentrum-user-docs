[Galaxy](https://galaxyproject.org/) is a web-based platform for accessible, reproducible, and transparent computational research. Used by researchers across the globe to access public infrastructure.

![pic-landing](/img/meta/galaxy/usegalaxy-landing-page.png)

Having originated in the bioinformatics community, Galaxy spans numerous scientific disciplines (ecology, chemistry, NLP, climate science, social sciences, …). Nowadays, thousands of computational tools are available, supported by dozens of scientific and software development teams.

The user community is worldwide, with main installations in the US, EU, and Australia, complemented with a network of national installations (usegalaxy.*).

The Czech national service [usegalaxy.cz](https://usegalaxy.cz) is provided by [e-INFRA CZ](https://e-infra.cz) / [MetaCentrum](https://metacentrum.cz) together with [ELIXIR CZ](https://www.elixir-czech.cz).

It provides thousands of tools from various scientific domains and offers large data quotas for Czech researchers and their collaborators.


## Accessing usegalaxy.cz

The web interface is available at https://usegalaxy.cz and requires users to log in with either `e-INFRA CZ` or `Life Science login`.

- e-INFRA CZ login is available to virtually all Czech academia members via their own institutional credentials. It comes with 250 GB storage space on usegalaxy.cz.
- [ELIXIR/Life‑Science](https://lifescience-ri.eu/ls-login/users/how-to-get-and-use-life-science-id.html) is even wider federation of institutions spanning basically the whole Europe and more. It comes with 50GB of storage quota.

### User Quotas

### Support requests

Please feel free to ask for more resources by emailing galaxy@cesnet.cz.

Describe your project's needs to us and we will assist with increasing your storage/compute/concurrency or other resource quotas temporarily.
We can also install more available tools, help you wrap a new tool for Galaxy, or collaborate on designing a workflow.

### FTP Upload – Step‑by‑Step

Because Galaxy’s federated log‑ins don’t provide an FTP password out of the box, you need to create one first.
Additional steps are required in order to obtain a password for your FTP access:

1. Log in to [usegalaxy.cz](https://usegalaxy.cz) using one of the federated login options. At this point, you don't have an FTP password yet.
2. Log out of Galaxy and go to the [Galaxy login](https://usegalaxy.cz/login/) panel and enter your registered email address into the username field.
3. Click on the `Click here to reset your password` button.
4. You will receive an email with the reset password link. Check your spam folder if necessary.
5. Click on the provided link and set up a new password for your FTP access.
6. Once you have set a new password, you can use your registered email address and the new password to log in to our ftp server at `usegalaxy.cz` and copy your files there.
7. Follow the data import process described in the [docs](https://galaxyproject.org/ftp-upload/).

<Callout type="info">
Note: The FTP password you set does not affect your Galaxy web login. Keep it safe, but you can reset it at any time via the same password-reset procedure.
</Callout>

### How to link multiple identities

If you have both e-infra and LSlogin identities you can link them in Galaxy. This prevents problems when switching between log-ins.

1. Click on *User* in the top menu
2. Select *Preferences*.
3. Select *Manage Third-Party Identities*.
4. Choose the other identity provider and authenticate yourself.
